Empowering a generation to confidently defend their faith through reason, evidence, and truth.

01

Building a community of confident Christians in Kerala, equipped with robust intellectual foundations to articulate and defend their faith in an increasingly skeptical world.

02

Engaging Modern Challenges

Thoughtfully addressing the rise of new atheism among Kerala's youth with compelling arguments, demonstrating Christianity as a coherent, evidence-based worldview that offers genuine hope and purpose.
